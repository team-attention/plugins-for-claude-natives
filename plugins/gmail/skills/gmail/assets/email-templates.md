# Email Body Templates

Select an appropriate template based on the situation when composing emails.

---

## 1. Meeting Request

```
Hello {recipient_name},

{Brief greeting or context}

I would like to discuss {meeting purpose}.

**Proposed Schedule:**
- {date1} {time1}
- {date2} {time2}

**Estimated Duration:** {30 minutes/1 hour}
**Location/Format:** {Office location / Google Meet / Zoom}

Please let me know your preferred time and I will confirm the schedule.

Thank you.
```

---

## 2. Information/Document Request

```
Hello {recipient_name},

Regarding {project/task name}, I need the following materials.

**Requested Items:**
1. {item1}
2. {item2}
3. {item3}

**Deadline:** By {date}

Please let me know if you have any questions.

Thank you.
```

---

## 3. Progress Update

```
Hello {recipient_name},

Here is the progress update for {project name}.

**Completed:**
- {completed item 1}
- {completed item 2}

**In Progress:**
- {in-progress item 1} (Expected completion: {date})

**Next Steps:**
- {next item}

**Issues/Blockers:**
- {issue or "None"}

Please let me know if further discussion is needed.

Thank you.
```

---

## 4. Thank You/Follow-up Email

```
Hello {recipient_name},

Thank you for the {meeting/call/assistance}.

{Brief summary or key points}

As discussed, I will proceed with {follow-up action}.

Please feel free to reach out if you have any additional questions.

Thank you.
```

---

## 5. Introduction Email

```
Hello {recipient_name},

I am reaching out through {introducer_name}.

I am {name}, {title} at {company/organization}.
{Brief self/company introduction}

{Purpose of contact and proposal}

Would you be available for a brief call or meeting?

Thank you.
```

---

## 6. Reminder

```
Hello {recipient_name},

I am following up on the email I sent on {previous email date} regarding {subject}.

{Brief summary or request}

I understand you are busy, but I would appreciate a response by {date}.

Thank you.
```

---

## Template Usage Guide

1. **Assess the situation**: Select an appropriate template based on context identified in Step 1 (Explore Tasks)
2. **Replace variables**: Replace `{variable}` parts with actual content
3. **Adjust tone**: Adjust formality level based on relationship with recipient
4. **Add signature**: Add signature from `assets/signatures.md` at the bottom
